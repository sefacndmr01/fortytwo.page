# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 6, 2026

Nyx Clock ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed and maintained by Fortytwo Apps. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, 
how it is used, and your rights as a user.

---

## 1. Summary

Nyx Clock is designed with privacy in mind. We do **not** collect, 
store, or share your personal data. All app settings and preferences 
are stored **locally on your device only**.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data Stored Locally (On Your Device)

The following data is saved on your device using local storage and 
is **never transmitted to us or any third party**:

- Selected clock face and visual preferences
- Customization settings (font, color, size, etc.)
- App language preference

This data is stored using your device's local storage 
(AsyncStorage) and is cleared when you uninstall the app.

### 2.2 Location Data (Optional — Weather Feature)

If you enable the **weather display** feature, the app will request 
access to your device's location. This is used **solely** to fetch 
local weather conditions.

- Location data is used in real-time to request weather information
- We use **Open-Meteo** (open-meteo.com), a privacy-friendly, 
  open-source weather API
- Your coordinates are sent directly to Open-Meteo servers; 
  we do not receive, log, or store your location
- Location access is **optional** — the app works fully without it
- You can revoke location permission at any time via device settings

Please refer to [Open-Meteo's Privacy Policy](https://open-meteo.com/en/terms) 
for how they handle data.

### 2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No account or login required
- No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No crash reporting services
- No third-party SDKs that collect data

---

## 3. Purchase & Payments

Nyx Clock is available as a **one-time purchase** through 
Google Play Store. All payment transactions are handled entirely 
by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment or 
billing information. Please refer to 
[Google Play's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) 
for details.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---|---|---|
| Google Play | App distribution & purchase | Handled by Google |
| Open-Meteo | Weather data (optional) | Approximate location (if enabled) |
| Google Fonts | Typefaces bundled at build time | None (no runtime requests) |

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Nyx Clock does not knowingly collect any data from children under 
the age of 13. The app contains no user accounts, social features, 
or data collection mechanisms.

---

## 6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not 
operate any servers or databases, there is no risk of a server-side 
data breach. Your preferences remain entirely under your control.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Access & Deletion**: Uninstalling the app removes all locally 
  stored data
- **Location**: Revoke permission at any time in device Settings → 
  Apps → Nyx Clock → Permissions
- **Contact**: For any privacy-related questions, contact us at 
  the address below
